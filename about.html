About INNERMEN EQUIPMENT

HISTORY

Innermen Equipment Company Limited launched in 2019 as an online hardware sales & marketing company engaged in the sales, lease, and rental of light-duty construction equipment, building materials and personal protection equipment. At Innermen, we market hardware as more than just materials and tools for the workplace, but as entities that trigger workers’ confidence and speed up job completion. Our mission is to help businesses and individuals get the best quality hardware at light speed. We do this by creatively leveraging technology to make videos that simplify technicalities and provide clear guide on the operation and installation procedure.
Whether it is personal or corporate project, Innermen services offer the fastest route to having your construction tools and materials on site. Our offering cuts across the building/construction industry. These include Roofing Materials, Ladders, Scaffolds, Scissors Lift, Fiber Cement Boards, Concrete Mixers, Disc Cutters, Site Lighting, Walk Behind Rollers, Compactor, Rammers, and all other accessories required in construction work. Our strategic objective is sustainable value creation through market leadership and differentiation.

PHILOSOPHY

The company has also been planning with financial foresight since its founding in 2019, this is geared towards ensuring our longterm continuity. Innermen purpose to build a better future – with sustainable and innovative solutions. The leadership value of integrity, speed, courage, continuous learning, teamwork, longterm orientation, diversity, and her embrace of emerging technologies are demonstrated in the everyday interactions with team members, partners, and customers. Our online marketplace, a one-stop shop, helps our customers to access all their hardware needs from the comfort of their home. Stemming from our online roots, we have further expanded into an omni-channel hardware provider.
We now offer seamless and direct services to DIYers/Artisans (Do-It-Yourself) and professional market with a collection of over 50,000 items that are globally sourced but locally rendered. By leveraging all our touchpoints, we showcase the best hardware without being lost on the importance of low-cost and high effectiveness.

VISION STATEMENT

Simplify technical complexity in the hardware market by providing the best fit equipment and ensuring speedy supply of products to the site.

MISSION STATEMENT

Bridge the knowledge gap between construction workers (engineers & artisans) and their work tools.
